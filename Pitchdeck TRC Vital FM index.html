05 — KPIs & Guardrails

How I know it's working. And when to pull the plug.

North star: Net Revenue Growth across the event portfolio. (your phrase, from the JD)

Diagnostic Order

How I read a broken dashboard

CTR → LP conversion → funnel CVR → CAC → ROAS

Problems fire top-of-funnel first. CTR breaks in days, ROAS breaks in weeks. Walk backwards to find where.

Importance Order

How success is judged

ROAS → CAC → funnel CVR

ROAS earns the budget. CAC proves the unit economics. Funnel CVR is the improvement lever.

The machine, and how it stays running.

Self-running does not mean zero human involvement. It means the machine runs on its own between defined check-ins. Data flows automatically. Alerts fire when guardrails hit. Creative and campaign work continues on a documented cadence. Your check-in frequency drops as trust in the system builds.
