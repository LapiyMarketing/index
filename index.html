North Star

Net Revenue Growth across the event portfolio.

CTR → LP conversion → funnel CVR → CAC → ROAS

ROAS → CAC → funnel CVR

Diagnose. Build. Decide.

PHASE 1 · MONTH 1
Communication: daily to every-other-day
Diagnose and stabilize.

Audit Work

  • Google Ads audit both brands, including Search Terms report on TRC
  • Meta Pixel installation audit across Wix and LearnWorlds
  • UTM parameter integrity end-to-end
  • Baseline LP conversion measurement, both brands
  • Mailchimp segmentation audit
  • SEO audit both brands
  • WhatsApp community architecture review

Team & Ops

  • Designer sync — video capability, AI creative comfort
  • Assistant sync — scope availability
  • Reporting layer stood up
  • Creative brief format and cadence
  • Reconciled baseline metrics report delivered

Launches

  • TRC Google restructured based on findings
  • Vital FM Google Search cold campaign

Does NOT launch

  • Vital FM Meta — waits for creative + tracking
  • Landing page rebuilds — measure first
  • Any change to what's currently working
End of Phase 1: revenue holds flat or improves marginally. The machine is ready to grow without breaking.
PHASE 2 · MONTH 2
Communication: 2x per week
Build the system.

Launches

  • Vital FM Meta prospecting with multi-step qualification LP
  • Meta-fed Google retargeting via GA4 audience sync
  • Customer email lists as Meta Custom Audiences + Google Customer Match
  • Vital FM landing page rebuild
  • Segmented email nurture sequences in Mailchimp

Cadence

  • 3–5 creative angles per cycle, 2-week evaluation
  • Assistant trained on lead tagging + qualification logging
  • Weekly Monday dashboard live
  • TRC changes small and incremental (measure 3x before adjusting)
End of Phase 2: direction on unit economics, first evidence of assist channel working, LP CVR uplift on Vital FM rebuild.
PHASE 3 · MONTH 3
Communication: 1x per week + monthly deep dive
Optimize and decide.

Optimize

  • Full campaign optimization on 60 days of data
  • Second LP test if Vital FM rebuild validated
  • TRC → Vital FM cross-sell automation live
  • TikTok Spark Ads exploration if audience overlap justifies

Decide

  • 90-day performance report — validated CAC, blended ROAS
  • Scale decision framework: $10K, $15K, $20K/mo
  • Documented operating playbook
  • Q4 planning: budget, roadmap, hire recommendation
End of Phase 3: scale-or-iterate decision on data, not hope. If unit economics validate, we scale. If they need another 30–60 days, that's the honest answer.

Reporting throughout: Weekly dashboard live for you to check anytime. Monthly 30–45 minute video call for strategic review. Day 90 QBR for the scale decision.

AI Comfort — Discussion

Every business has a different tolerance for AI in production. I use AI for creative iteration, ad copy variants, and analysis. Your team's comfort shapes what I can produce quickly vs what stays fully human-generated.

  • AI-generated ad copy variants — okay to test, or human-written only?
  • AI image generation for social — comfortable, or brand risk?
  • AI video tools for TikTok cuts — worth exploring, or off-limits?

The Hire Question — Day 60 Answer, Not Day 0

You asked what to hire outside the marketing budget. That answer requires visibility I won't have until Phase 1 is complete. By day 60, the constraints will be clear — creative production, technical implementation, or customer response — and a specific proposal with cost and justification will follow.

Categories to evaluate against: media buyer, video editor, copywriter, CRO specialist, attribution analyst. No more than one hire in the first 90 days.
